How we deliver your automation project

A structured, collaborative approach – from early scoping through to training and support – reduces surprises on site and keeps risk under control.

  1. 1

    Discover

    Gather P&IDs, single-lines, control narratives, I/O lists and network drawings. Clarify operational constraints and shutdown windows.

  2. 2

    Design

    Define architecture, select platforms, design panels and control strategies, and agree standards for graphics, naming and alarms.

  3. 3

    Build & test

    Develop code and graphics, assemble panels, and perform FAT with your team – resolving issues before equipment goes to site.

  4. 4

    Commission & support

    Site installation, SAT, tuning and handover, followed by training and ongoing support for operations and maintenance teams.
