Human Machine Interface for modern plants

HMI is the primary window between operators and the process – providing intuitive screens, alarms, trends and controls so teams can monitor and act quickly.

  • Single machine panels to plant-wide HMI/SCADA
  • New projects, retrofits and platform migrations
  • Integration with PLC, SCADA, DCS and historians

What a good HMI should deliver

  • Clear, high-contrast graphics and status indication
  • Logical navigation and fast access to critical screens
  • Alarm pages with priorities and clear messages
  • Trending, diagnostics and maintenance views
  • Rugged hardware suitable for industrial environments
