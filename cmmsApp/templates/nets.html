Why variable speed drives?

Instead of running motors at constant full speed, variable speed drives regulate motor speed and torque. This improves process control, reduces energy consumption and extends equipment life.

  • Significant energy savings on pumps, fans, compressors and conveyors
  • Smoother starting and stopping, with reduced mechanical shock and noise
  • Improved control of flow, pressure, level and speed in your process

What a good VSD solution should deliver

  • Correctly sized drive and enclosure for your motor and environment
  • Compliance with site standards (IEC / NEMA) and utility requirements
  • Harmonic, EMC and motor insulation considerations engineered in
  • Easy integration with PLC, DCS and SCADA systems

SIEMENS – SINAMICS

SINAMICS enclosed and modular drives

SINAMICS enclosed drives are based on Siemens’ Totally Integrated Automation concept, combining modular hardware with powerful software tools. We engineer SINAMICS solutions around your load profile, network and enclosure requirements (IEC or NEMA).

  • SINAMICS G120E – configurable enclosed LV drives in NEMA 1 or 12
  • SINAMICS G150 – air- or water-cooled enclosed drives (non-regenerative)
  • SINAMICS S120 CM – common DC bus systems and multi-motor line-ups
  • SINAMICS S150 – regenerative AC/AC drives for high-performance duties
ALLEN-BRADLEY – PowerFlex

PowerFlex medium voltage drive systems

Allen-Bradley PowerFlex drives provide flexible, efficient control of medium-voltage motors in critical industrial applications. Systems can be supplied with arc-resistant ArcShield technology and regeneration.

  • PowerFlex 6000 – MV AC drives for 2.3–11 kV fans, pumps & compressors
  • PowerFlex 7000 – air- and liquid-cooled MV drives up to 34,000 HP
  • PowerFlex 7000 with ArcShield – arc-resistant MV drive system with full regeneration
ABB – ACS800 & ACS880

ABB all-compatible industrial drives

ABB’s ACS800 and ACS880 drives bring safety, reliability and flexibility to process industries and OEM applications. We support complete cabinet systems as well as drive modules for your own panel designs.

  • ACS800 single drives – proven wall-mounted and cabinet-built drives
  • ACS880 single drives & drive modules – all-compatible platform for new systems
  • ACS880 liquid-cooled drives – direct liquid cooling, 98% heat removal, fully enclosed
  • ACS880 flange-mounted drives – ACS880-01/-11/-31 and -04F/-04FXT for harsh environments
