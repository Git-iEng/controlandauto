What do we mean by IoT modules?

IoT modules and gateways sit between your field devices and IT/OT systems, collecting data from sensors, PLCs and meters and securely publishing it to dashboards, analytics tools and business applications.

  • Connect legacy PLCs, drives and instruments without replacing them
  • Standardise and expose process data for reporting and analytics
  • Enable remote monitoring, alarms and digital services for your plant

What a good IoT solution should deliver

  • Industrial-grade hardware designed for 24/7 operation
  • Support for common fieldbuses and protocols (Modbus, Profinet, OPC UA, etc.)
  • Hardened network design and cyber-security controls built-in
  • Clean data models that your engineers can easily work with

SIEMENS – SIMATIC IOT2000 / IOT2050

Edge gateways for connecting machines and sensors

Siemens SIMATIC IOT2000 and IOT2050 devices are industrial-grade IoT gateways based on open Linux platforms. They read data from PLCs, sensors and serial/legacy equipment and publish it to higher-level systems.

  • Support for industrial protocols and fieldbuses
  • Pre-processing, buffering and filtering of data at the edge
  • Integration with MindSphere and third-party applications
  • Mounting and enclosures suitable for control panels and plant rooms
SIEMENS – MindSphere & cloud IoT

Industrial IoT-as-a-service for your assets

MindSphere and other cloud IoT platforms provide a scalable environment for collecting, storing and analysing time-series data from multiple plants and sites.

  • Secure onboarding of assets and data sources
  • KPI dashboards, alarms and notifications for operations teams
  • Analytics and app frameworks for custom use cases
  • APIs for integration with CMMS, ERP and other business systems
